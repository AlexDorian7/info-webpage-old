Thee Omega's DMing Services website proposal

Snapshot

Adam has over 40 years experience at Dungeon Matering for Dungeons and Dragons. He is looking for a first time website for his D&D groups that he heads. Adam needs a website that attrach potential new players while providing the information needed to the returning ones.

Business Needs

The website needs to fulfil the following business needs:

  1. Reach a wider base of D&D fans to widen his base of DMing opening up more opprotunities for campaigns and other table time.

Audience Needs

The target audience for Adam's website is defined as potential and returning D&D players
The website needs to assist the target audience to do the following:

  1. Assist in finding a compatible group.
  2. Assist in creating or forming a new group.
  3. Assisting in answering any questions that a new group may have with Official dungeons and drgaons information.

The website should be a useful resource for existing players, potential new players, and potential new partners.

Solution

I recommend the development of a website from scratch that will show the value Adam's Campaigns add to the value of its members.

The new website will be designed to:

  • Inform the target audience of who Adam is and the services he offers
  • Provide the target audience with information on how to contact Adam for more details on his services
  • Provide the target audience with a common space for information about Adam and the games he runs

The site will be made from scratch using some frameworks as a base. The site will employ modern day tools and skills to create a reactive webpage that conveys the information needed while looking pleasing with a unique style.

Site Description

This site will contain five pages as folows

Page Name Page Description
Home/Welcome This page will contain general Info about Adam and what he does.
About This page Will contain more info about Adam and the games he has run in the past as well as his experience and the experience his groups have.
Contact This page will contain contact information for Adam.
Product Info This page will contain specific information on the services Adam offers.
Testmonials This page will contain testmonials from previous and current players in Adam's games.
